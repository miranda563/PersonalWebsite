    Hi, my name is Jenalyn, and I am currently in my third year of college. I have been at Batangas State University for 3 years now. The school is great, the people are fantastic, and the atmosphere is one that makes you actually want to go to school. Before I joined BSU, I studied at Lemery Colleges for two years. I grew up in Agoncillo Batangas, where I was born and raised. I am an interesting, fun loving guy with a good sense of humor. One thing about me that is important to know though is that at first I come off as a very shy individualistic girl but once I get to know people and they are comfortable with my environment I am great. It is hard for me to make friends but once I do, it’s great and I am all different. I joke, laugh, and humor people and I also get as much as I give. I am a very kind, compassionate, and sensitive girl as my close friends will tell you. I have a tough shell but on the inside I am soft. I am also a very principled person and stand strong for what is right and wrong. I am also a very straight-forward and honest person. I understand and know that we all put on a show sometimes and do not show who we really are or what we feel but some people do this constantly and those kinds of people I cannot stand. Meeting new people who support my efforts to grow and develop my skills not only in subjects but also in my hobbies provides me with a lot of energy. I’m in love with photography, so the thing I love doing so much became even more enjoyable. I decided to take beautiful pictures of accidental people and share these photos with them by emails or in social media. I feel happiness inside seeing how happy they are looking at the pictures where they are walking or sitting somewhere in the park.
